Applying for Membership:

To become a member, please contact the ISUD Secretary, Robert Allinson, via email at: rallinson@soka.edu with a short bio. There is no entry fee in becoming a member.

Annual Membership Fee:

The annual membership fee is 10 USD, or equivalent, per year. Membership fees much be paid at least every two years, for a total of $20 USD or equivalent.

The membership fee can be paid through the ISUD website using PayPal, credit card, or bank transfer. To pay your membership fee, click the "Donate" button below.
